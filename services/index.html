Auto Hail Damage Repair

At VIVID Auto Body Shop, we also offer professional Auto Hail Damage Repair services.  Whether it requires paintless dent repair (PDR) or conventional paint and body repair for vehicles with more extensive damages or dents that are not repairable using the paintless dent repair method.

We consider ourselves a “PDR First” auto body shop and hail repair center. What that means is that we always consider paintless dent repair (PDR) as the first method to repair dents from hail, door dings, and minor accidents. PDR far exceeds the limitations of traditional dent repair and keeps cars in their original condition. In fact, PDR is an avenue that most insurance companies prefer because it is less expensive and typically a quicker repair turnaround.

Our auto hail repair technicians are highly experienced utilizing state of the art tools and repair procedures. PDR is a great option when your vehicle has sustained body damage but with the paint is still intact / not cracked or compromised. Paintless dent removal is generally less expensive compared to traditional auto body repair and paint because the amount of labor hours are greatly reduced and no paint materials are involved. Another added bonus using PDR as a repair technique, repair time and turnaround are much faster.

Unlike many fly-by-night competitors who come to North Texas only when there is a hailstorm and leave as soon as they’ve sufficiently lined their pockets, VIVID Auto Body & Hail Damage Repair has been in the Dallas area since 1999.  We will be around for the long haul, standing behind our work and our customers backed by a Lifetime Warranty that is valid as long as you own the vehicle.  At VIVID Auto Body Shop, we will clearly explain all your choices to address your vehicle damages and if PDR is the best option for you, we will provide awesome results.
