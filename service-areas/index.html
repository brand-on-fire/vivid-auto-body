WHY CHOOSE VIVID AUTO BODY & HAIL DAMAGE REPAIR?

Our Quality Workmanship

With over 100-years of combined automotive repair experience, the staff at VIVID Auto Body has established itself as the leader in customer satisfaction and quality repairs. Our I-Car® Certified and experienced technicians take pride in the quality of their work and craftsmanship. From basic dents and dings to auto hail repair to extensive frame repair, our staff has the skills necessary to handle any repair. This is why many insurance companies trust us to serve their valued customers.

Crystal Clear Communication

Throughout the repair process we not only communicate the progress, we also educate our customers of what services are performed to restore their car into pre-accident condition. Additionally, we work with our customers to try to accomplish the most cost-effective repair possible without compromising the quality of the workmanship. We are here to help you get back on the road!

Our Equipment & Paint

We are excited and proud to announce that we use a state of the art paint booths! We use the same paint booths that are in use at large dealerships and other well-known auto body repair shops. Additionally, we only use the highest quality materials made by R&M BASF, PPG, 3M, AutoMagic, and Evercoat.

High-Quality Replacement Parts

We are a strong advocate of using Original and Brand New OEM Parts for the highest quality of repairs. We always work and negotiate with insurance companies to cover using Brand New OEM parts. If the repair project is out-of-pocket, we will work closely with our customer’s budget and provide the best recommendations.

Locally & Privately Owned

We are a locally owned and operated auto body & hail repair shop in McKinney, TX. We are not a fly-by-night establishment. VIVID Auto Body will be around for the long haul, standing behind our work and our customers backed by a Lifetime Warranty. Our standards are set in-house, not by anyone else, which allows us to go the extra mile on your repair.

Lifetime Warranty

VIVID Auto Body issues a Limited Lifetime Warranty with each repair we perform. Our Repair Policy guarantees against defects in workmanship for as long the customer owns the repaired vehicle. Parts and materials are subject to the manufacturers specific warranties.
