Your McKinney Auto Body Shop Experts. How can we help you?

VIVID Auto Body Shop, a top-rated, I-Car® Gold Certified, family-owned premier auto collision and hail repair center in McKinney, Texas.

Our auto body repair services include Hail Damage Repair (Paintless Dent Repair (PDR) and Conventional), Collision Repair with Mechanical Repair, Custom Widebody Conversions and Body Kit Installation, Custom Painting on all domestic and foreign vehicles and motorcycles.  We also install Paint Protection Film (PPF aka Clear Bra) and Vinyl Wrap (glossy, matte, or custom printed) on both commercial fleet vehicles and individual vehicles.  Contact us today and let us know how we can help you.

We are proud to serve areas in North Texas such as McKinney, Allen, Princeton, Anna, Melissa, Van Alstyne, Wylie, Farmersville, Murphy, Plano, Frisco, Garland, Denton, Greenville, Richardson, and Prosper.

We are 4 miles east of I-75 and Highway 380.
